About Me

I'm just a simple man with a thirst for knoweldge. I grew up in the suburbs of Long Island and ended up going to school at Marist College to study advertising. Things didn't work out to well for me after that though as I started working in a completly different field as a production supervisor at a culinary warehouse. This was not the path I was meant for and I lived with a sense of unfulfillment everyday.

I started the Web Development Immersive program at General Assembly in Febuary of 2015 and I finally felt like I was putting my mind to good use. I learned a tremendous amount about coding in those 3 months and now I am ready to put my new found skills to work.
